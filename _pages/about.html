About me

I postdoctoral researcher in mathematics at the University of Bonn. I completed my PhD at the University of Southampton in 2024, under the supervision of Ashot Minasyan. My field is geometric group theory, with my main objects of study being groups that satisfy various nonpositive curvature conditions. I am interested in trying to understand the relationship between algebraic and geometric properties of subgroups in nonpositively curved groups.
